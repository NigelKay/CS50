What is Bonsai?

Bonsai is an East Asian art form which utilizes cultivation techniques to produce, in pots, small trees that mimic the scale and shape of full sized trees.

Purposes of bonsai are primarily for the pleasure of the grower and also for the contemplation for the viewer. In contrast to other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is not intended for production of food or medicine. Rather, Bonsai practice focuses on the long-term growing and shaping of one or more trees in a pot.

Types of Bonsai

There are many types of popular Bonsai. A few key types include: tropical, sub-tropical, temperate and hardy trees.

Difficulty of Bonsai

While Bonsai has a reputation of being a difficult hobby, with the right care and attention, it can be easily managable. Check out our care guide for more tips!
